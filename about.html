About Volunteer Connect

WHO WE ARE?

Land Acknowledgement

Volunteer Connect York Region acknowledges that the land on which we operate is the traditional territory of many nations including the Mississaugas of the Credit, the Anishnabeg, the Chippewa, the Haudenosaunee, and the Wendat peoples, and is now home to many diverse First Nations, Inuit, and Métis peoples. We also acknowledge that Markham, Richmond Hill, and Vaughan are covered by Treaty 13 with the Mississaugas of the Credit and the Williams Treaties signed with multiple Mississaugas and Chippewa bands.

About Us

At Volunteer Connect, we strive to connect our community members with programs, services and opportunities that helps them to contribute and make meaningful connections that foster inclusion and a sense of belonging. We embrace and reflect the rich and diverse composition of the York region community.

Our Mission

We provide south York Region residents and volunteer organizations with leadership and resources to promote volunteerism and enrich the lives of those in our community. Volunteer Connect’s Registered Charitable Number: 118968338 RR0001 pexels-mikhail-nilov-8543581
